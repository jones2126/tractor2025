# DROK 12V to 5V Buck Converter

## Product

- **Amazon:** https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0D141CZMZ
- **Dimensions:** 65.5mm × 29.2mm

## Usage on Electronics Board

Two DROK buck converters are mounted on the electronics board:

- **Buck converter #1** — Powers the RPi 5 via USB-C output
- **Buck converter #2** — Powers the USB hub (BIG7) via Micro-USB output

## Notes

- Input: 12V from tractor battery
- Output: 5V
- Both converters need clearance for tight wire bends on the board
- A 3D-printed retention strap/shroud is planned for securing them (Fusion 360 model exists)
