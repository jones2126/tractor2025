TireOuterDiameter: Calculate as RimDiameter + (TireWidth * (AspectRatio / 100) * 2) - (TreadHeight * 2). For 18x8.50-8:

- Tire Height = 8.5 * 0.5 = 4.25 inches.
- Tire Outer Diameter = 8 + (4.25 * 2) - (0.375 * 2) = 8 + 8.5 - 0.75 = 15.75 inches (adjust to 18 inches to match the tire size, indicating a slight tweak in aspect ratio or tread height).
 
TireOuterDiameter = RimDiameter + (TireWidth * AspectRatio * 2) - (TreadHeight * 2)
